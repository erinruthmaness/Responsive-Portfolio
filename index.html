About Me

Originally from Burlington, NC, after majoring in French at UNC Chapel Hill I spent two years living in Paris and two in Manhattan before finally moving to Charlotte, NC. I spent my 20s working enjoyable but unfulfilling jobs in publishing and in insurance before deciding to attend the UNC Charlotte full stack coding boot camp in order to learn a new and interesting skill that will help me build a career in my 30s.
